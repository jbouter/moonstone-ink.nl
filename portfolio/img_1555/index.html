Moonstone Ink

My name is Emmely. I live in Groningen. I started tattooing in 2020. I’ve been drawing since I was able to hold a pencil 😌 Learning how to tattoo felt like drawing with a new tool on skin instead of paper 🌝

Last year I realized my ultimate dream; opening my own tattoo shop. I’m the proud owner of Tattoo studio Moonstone Ink 🌙 It’s located in Groningen at the Travertijnstraat 6.

I came up with the name Moonstone Ink for my business for a reason. I wear moonstones in most of my jewelry and felt a connection with them for a while 💎

For my logo used a picture of my own hand holding my tattoo machine. Moonstones are connected to feminine energy, stands for self awareness, stimulates creativity and helps you through personal changes ✨

It felt like a perfect match for this new journey in my life. I also give my customers a moonstone as a little gift after their tattoo session 🌙

This page contains a small set of my portfolio, for more work please see my instagram.
